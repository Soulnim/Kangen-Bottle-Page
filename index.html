KANGEN BOTTLE

What is Kangen Bottle?

Your "All-In-One" future water bottle that specialises to convert your drinking water into healthy alkaline water.

FEATURES

Convert

...

Kangen Bottle helps to convert your drinking water into healthy alkaline water with the help of the converting technology built by Enagic that is currently used in the SD501, an alkaline water machine, and other Enagic products.

Filter

...

Kangen Bottle can be used to filter your drinking water. This feature makes the bottle useful to bring while travelling and hiking. With a single push of a button, the water was immediately filtered and ready to be consumed.

Boil

...

Kangen Bottle has a built-in heater, allowing you to reheat or boil water wherever you are. It is an ideal feature that you can use to reheat your cold coffee while you're working, too.

Check

...

You can obtain more precise information about your drinking water with the tip of your finger. Besides, you can monitor the Kangen Bottle's process with your phone too.
